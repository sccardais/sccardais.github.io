About GreenHeart Initiative

We're a community-driven non-profit dedicated to enhancing our downtown park's beauty and functionality for all residents.

Our Mission

To create and maintain a beautiful, accessible, and sustainable urban park that serves as the heart of our community, fostering connections between people and nature.

Our Vision

We envision a downtown park that is vibrant year-round, with lush greenery, engaging public art, and spaces that invite community gatherings, recreation, and relaxation.
